D2L Grade Transfer

Make sure that all student grades are ready to be transferred. Then run the following actions in oder.

1. Login to D2L. This will open a new window.

2. Begin Grade Transfer

3. Wait for Email

Once the transfer is complete, you will receive an email with the results.

4. Download record

Once the transfer is complete, you can download the record of the transfer. This will show you any errors that may have occurred.
